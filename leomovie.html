About the movie :

Leo is a 2023 Indian action thriller starring Vijay, produced by Seven Screen Studio, and directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The film follows a cafe owner in Shimla, who is revealed to be a former gangster named Leo Das, as he is pursued by a drug cartel who believe he is the estranged son of a menacing gangster. The film is the third installment in the Lokesh Cinematic Universe.
