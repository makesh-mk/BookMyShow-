About the movie :

Kaththi (2014) is a Tamil action-drama about an escaped convict, Kathiresan (Vijay), who assumes the identity of his lookalike, a social activist named Jeevanantham (also Vijay), to fight a greedy multinational corporation trying to seize a village's fertile land for commercial projects, highlighting issues like farmers' suicides, water scarcity, capitalism, and corporate greed.
