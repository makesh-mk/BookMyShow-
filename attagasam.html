About the movie :

Attagasam (2004) is a Tamil action-drama about twin brothers, Jeeva and Guru, separated as kids by their mother to save them from their father's killer, growing up with contrasting lives (Jeeva as a driving instructor, Guru as a gangster in Thoothukudi), who later reunite with unexpected swap-ins, all packed with action, sentiment, and Ajith Kumar's dual-role swagger.
